How to use

  • Write down the task to focus on.
  • Work on this task with undivided attention for 25 minutes. Try not to check your phone, your email, or anything else during this time.
  • After 25 minutes of work, give yourself a 5 minute break. Check your phone, meditate, or do whatever you need to do to rest your mind. Don't work on the task.
  • Repeat the above steps. Every time you finish a cycle, write a checkmark down (or use the track below). Every four cycles, reward yourself with a longer 15 minute break.
